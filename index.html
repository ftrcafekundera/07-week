About Me

Originally from Chicago, Illinois, Peter currently resides in the Washington D.C. metropolitan area, where he works as a multilingual geopolitical research analyst for a due diligence and risk management consulting firm. Prior to his current position, Peter was living in Beijing, China, where he completed his undergraduate and graduate studies; he obtained a master’s degree in comparative literature and world literature from Peking University as well as bachelor’s degrees in teaching Mandarin as a second language and international business from Minzu University, graduating as class valedictorian.

---

Peter has worked in fast-paced environments throughout his entire corporate career. From news agencies to consulting firms, he has thrived in deadline-oriented industries. He possesses more than five years of professional writing, translation, and editing experience, along with years of collegiate and professional OSINT foreign language research skills. Moreover, he is familiar with domestic and foreign social media platforms, databases, and marketing strategies.

---

With his background in editing, passion for the written word, fluency in multiple languages (including Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, as well as European and Brazilian Portuguese, among others), high level of organization, team-oriented mentality, strict work ethic, and careful attention to detail, he is always prepared to meet any obstacle that comes my way. Lastly, he perceives every challenge as a learning experience and consider himself a lifelong learner.
